Newest Football Card Releases
2022 Sage Artistry

The 2022 SAGE Artistry football card set consists of 120 cards. It contains cards of the top rookies and also some top college stars. Some of the players include Kenny Pickett, C.J. Stroud, Breece Hall, Bijan Robinson and more.

2022 Panini Instant Supernova

The 2022 Panini Instant Supernova set consists of 24 cards. It includes cards of Patrick Mahomes, Tom Brady, Justin Herbert and rookies such as Kenny Pickett, Malik Willis, Drake London and other stars and rookies. Only 1,523 of each card were printed.

2022 Score

The 2022 Score football card set consists of 400 cards. There are 300 veteran and legends cards and 100 rookie cards. Top rookies include Kenny Pickett, Bailey Zappe, Breece Hall, Chris Olave and many others.

2021 Select

The 2021 Select football card set consists of 425 cards with different levels of rarity. Top rookies include Trevor Lawrence, Justin Fields, Najee Harris and more.

2022 Panini Studio Rookies

The 2022 Panini Instant Studio Rookies set consists of 42 cards. It has the top rookies of the 2022 class including Kenny Pickett, Bailey Zappe and more. Only 911 cards were produced of each player.

2022 Prestige

The 2022 Prestige football card set consists of 400 cards. There are 300 base veteran and legends cards and 100 rookie cards. Top rookies include Kenny Pickett, Breece Hall, Drake London, Chris Olave and many more.
